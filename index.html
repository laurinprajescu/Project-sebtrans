Who we are
 
Seb Trans Services Ltd is a newly formed company and its owner used to be an HGV Driver, having years of experience in the transport industry. Seb Trans Services Ltd is in continuous expansion looking forward to improving the quality of their services and to join the other companies in the competitive transport industry. Our goal is to offer an impeccable service to the customers.
Being a small company we have the advantage of a more straightforward communication between departments and the ability to resolve issues more rapidly leading to a more accurate and clean service. Meeting the demands of clients in the modern world of transportation is our primary goal, despite any negative factors that day-to-day work encounters.
Our main activity is container transportation from Dartford port. We have the knowledge and the capability to deliver your container everytime on time.
